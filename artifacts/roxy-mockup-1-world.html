Who Is Roxy?

Who's Roxy? is a question we get asked a lot. The truth is, we're still figuring it out — and that's exactly the point. Born out of late-night rehearsals and too much coffee, we are a band built on raw energy, honest noise, and the kind of music that feels like it shouldn't exist yet.

Based in London, we've been tearing up stages across the UK since 2024. Our sound sits somewhere between post-punk urgency and experimental electronics — always shifting, never settling.

We're just getting started. Get loud with us.
