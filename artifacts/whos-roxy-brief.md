# WHO'S ROXY? — Band Website Project Brief

## What We're Building

Official website for **Who's Roxy?**, a new Dublin-based post-punk/electronic band. Priority is simplicity — social media links, band photos, and upcoming gigs. Think landing page more than deep site.

---

## Brand Identity

- Logo is spray-paint graffiti style — "WHO'S ROXY?" in magenta/hot pink with white glow effects on pure black
- **Core palette:**
  - `#000000` — Black (background)
  - `#CC00CC` / `#FF00FF` — Magenta / Hot Pink (primary accent)
  - `#FFFFFF` — White with glow (highlights)
- **Tone:** raw, kinetic, underground — NOT polished pop

---

## Design Direction (Gorillaz-Inspired)

We researched the top innovative band websites (Gorillaz, Radiohead, NIN, Björk, Chemical Brothers, Arcade Fire etc.) and ran 3 concept mockups. All 3 share:

- Dark/black background with magenta accent
- Custom cursor, grain/noise overlay, CRT scanlines or glow effects
- Animated elements (spray drips, equalizer bars, city backdrop, glitch text)
- Bottom or side navigation

---

## 3 Concepts Explored

### 01 — The World *(Gorillaz-style)*
Immersive scene with animated city background, perspective grid floor, glow splatters. Panel-based navigation (Gigs, Photos, About) slides in over the world. Socials in a vertical side dock.
> *Vibe: you enter the band's universe*

### 02 — The Wall *(Brutalist Zine)*
Bold editorial split layout — giant spray-painted type left, next gig info right. Running magenta ticker tape, numbered gig list, horizontal scrolling photo strip.
> *Vibe: a flyer that came to life*

### 03 — The Signal *(Glitchy Terminal)*
CRT monitor / underground rave aesthetic. Glitch animations on title, live audio equalizer visualizer, scanlines overlay, everything framed as a terminal interface. Sidebar holds next show + gig list + socials.
> *Vibe: a pirate radio station broadcasting live*

---

## Tech Stack (Current Mockups)

- Pure **HTML / CSS / JS** — no framework yet
- **Google Fonts:** Bebas Neue, Space Mono, Archivo Black, DM Mono, VT323, Rajdhani
- **CSS techniques:** keyframe animations, glitch effects, ticker tape, equalizer bars, perspective grid
- Custom cursor, scanlines, grain overlays, CSS clip-path shapes
- All mockups are **desktop-first** — mobile responsiveness still needed
